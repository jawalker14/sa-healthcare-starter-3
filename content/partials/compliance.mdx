# Compliance Information

This website is designed to comply with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). 

## HPCSA Compliance

We adhere to the ethical guidelines and standards set forth by the HPCSA to ensure the highest quality of care and professionalism in our practice. 

## POPIA Compliance

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the POPIA. Your data will be handled with the utmost care and will only be used for the purposes for which it was collected. 

For more information on our compliance practices, please refer to our privacy policy.
